Sir Reginald Honkington

Sir Reginald Honkington, the Goose Knight of the Gilded Lily, hails from a realm where ponds are made of liquid starlight and the reeds whisper ancient secrets. Clad in armor forged from moonbeams and river stones, he is on a quest to find the Legendary Golden Crumb, a relic said to grant infinite wisdom and perfectly toasted bread.
