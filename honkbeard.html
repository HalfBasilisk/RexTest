Captain Honkbeard

The infamous sky-pirate, Captain Honkbeard, terrorizes the clouds with his flock of loyal goose-mates. His ship, 'The Feathered Fury', is the fastest in the skies, powered by stolen thunder and pure, unadulterated honks. He is searching for the legendary treasure of the Cloud King, a hoard of golden crackers and enchanted pondweed.
