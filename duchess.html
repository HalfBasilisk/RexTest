Duchess Quacksworth

The Duchess Quacksworth of the Whispering Willow is a sorceress of great renown, her feathers shimmering with arcane energy. She commands the very waters, weaving spells that can calm the most turbulent storms or summon a gentle rain of breadcrumbs. Her familiar, a grumpy but loyal snail named Sheldon, carries her spellbook upon his shell.
