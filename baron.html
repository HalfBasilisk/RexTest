Baron von Goose

Baron von Goose is an intrepid explorer and cartographer, his wings stained with the ink of a thousand maps. He has charted the uncharted, from the floating islands of the Sky-Lakes to the subterranean rivers of the Under-Bog. He is currently seeking a crew for his airship, 'The Honking Wanderer', for a voyage to the edge of the world.
