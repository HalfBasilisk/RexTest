Lady Gertrude Feathers

Lady Gertrude Feathers is a celestial bard, her voice a chorus of starlight and her honk a symphony of the cosmos. It is said that the constellations themselves shift to hear her melodies. She carries a lute carved from a fallen star, its strings woven from the fabric of spacetime.
